I have been studying and playing music most of my life and I am fascinated by technology and its relationship to music and sound. I specialise in composition and sound design and have worked in a large variety of creative and technical contexts allowing me to develop my skills as a composer, sound designer and producer.

I have worked in various contexts, including producing singles and albums for artists and bands, audio post-production for film, and composing for screen and software development.

I am currently completing my Master's Degree in Sound Design at the University of Edinburgh and will be a graduate in September 2023.
